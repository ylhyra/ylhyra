## Overview for entering card data

Chars:

- Minor separator: `;`
- Major separator: `;;`
- Comma substitute: `∆`

Þú:

- `farð{{u}}`
- `kemur{{ðu}}`

Stíll:

- `{{gray|bla bla}}`
- `texti (note: aths)`
- `texti *fill in the blank here*`
- `'''feitletrun'''`
- `%` – base form is also alt id
- {{spp}} – "Same in past and present"
- `^orð` verður grátt
- `_orð_` verður grátt
