## Cards

Cards have the following attributes:

- front
- back
- direction
  - ->
  - <-
- importance – "-1" for extremely unimportant cards, "1" for particularly important cards
- level (optional) – Marks cards as being for a particular CEFR level (1-6)

Chars:

- Minor seperator: `;`
- Major seperator: `;;`
- Comma substitute: `∆`
