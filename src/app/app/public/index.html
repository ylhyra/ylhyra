Vocabulary

A simple flashcard game that uses spaced repetition to assist you in building a deeper active vocabulary. Intended to be be used daily, the game’s 2,000 words and sentences focus on giving you a feel for idiomatic language use and grammar. Suitable for beginners up to the B2 level. Includes 400 audio recordings.

Course

The course is an explanatory companion to the vocabulary game and covers the first 20% of the beginner (A1) level. The course is aimed at self-learners and introduces grammatical concepts slowly.
Ylhýra is free to use, but creating an account allows you to save your vocabulary progress. An account is available on a pay-what-you-want basis.
